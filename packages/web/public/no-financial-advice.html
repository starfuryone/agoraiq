§ 05
High Risk, Volatility & Leverage

Cryptocurrency markets can be extremely volatile. Prices may move rapidly and unpredictably. Trading on margin or using leverage can amplify both gains and losses, and may result in losses exceeding your initial investment.

Volatility MARKET

Sudden price gaps, thin liquidity, and cascading liquidations can produce losses that occur too quickly to manage manually.

Leverage DERIVATIVES

Leverage magnifies exposure. Even small adverse moves can trigger liquidation or significant drawdowns.

Execution OPS

Slippage, latency, outages, API errors, exchange halts, and liquidation engines can materially change outcomes versus paper results.

Counterparty EXCHANGE

Exchanges and custodians may fail, restrict withdrawals, become insolvent, be hacked, or be subject to enforcement actions.
