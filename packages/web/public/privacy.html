Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025 · ChatLogic Insights LTD

AgoraIQ ("we", "us", "our") is operated by ChatLogic Insights LTD. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

We collect information you provide when creating an account (name, email address, password), information from connected third-party accounts (Discord), payment information processed securely by Stripe, and usage data and analytics to improve our service.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data to provide and improve our services, process payments and manage subscriptions, send you service-related communications, personalise your experience, and comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with trusted service providers (Stripe for payments, hosting providers) under strict confidentiality agreements. We may disclose data if required by law.

Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Signal proof records are retained indefinitely as part of our immutable audit trail. You may request deletion of personal data subject to legal retention requirements.

Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. See our Cookie Policy for details.

Your Rights

Under GDPR and applicable law, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at privacy@agoraiq.net to exercise your rights.
