The Proof Problem
The crypto signal industry is rife with manipulation. Providers post only their winners and quietly delete losers. We built an immutable SHA-256 hashing system that makes this impossible on AgoraIQ.

Volatility-Adjusted Scoring
Not all wins are equal. A 10% gain in a low-volatility market is very different from a 10% gain when BTC moves 20% in a day. Our vol-adjusted scoring normalises for this.
