Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2025

AgoraIQ uses cookies to provide essential platform functionality and improve your experience.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and maintain sessions.

Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These are required for the platform to function and cannot be disabled:

  • session — Maintains your authentication session
  • csrf_token — Protects against cross-site request forgery

Analytics Cookies

We use anonymised analytics to understand how the platform is used. These can be disabled without affecting core functionality.

Managing Cookies

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies will prevent you from logging in.
