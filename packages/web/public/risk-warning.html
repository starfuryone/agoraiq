Risk Warning

ChatLogic Insights LTD · AgoraIQ
⚠ Cryptocurrency trading involves a high level of risk and is not suitable for all investors. You could lose some or all of your invested capital.

High Risk Nature of Crypto Trading

Cryptocurrencies are highly volatile assets. Prices can move dramatically in very short periods of time. The use of leverage amplifies both gains and losses significantly.

Signal Risk

Even highly-rated signal providers experience losing streaks. No signal provider has a 100% win rate. Following signals does not guarantee profitability and carries the risk of substantial losses.

Only Risk What You Can Afford to Lose

You should never trade with money you cannot afford to lose. Do not trade with borrowed money, money needed for living expenses, or money allocated to other important purposes.

No Guarantee of Returns

Historical performance data shown on AgoraIQ does not guarantee similar future performance. Market conditions change, and strategies that worked historically may not work in the future.

Regulatory Risk

Cryptocurrency regulation varies by jurisdiction and is evolving rapidly. Changes in regulation may affect the legality or practicality of crypto trading in your region.

Seek Independent Advice

If you are uncertain, seek independent financial advice from a qualified professional in your jurisdiction before making trading decisions.
