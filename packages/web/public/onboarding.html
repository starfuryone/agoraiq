FAQ

Is it free to get listed?
Yes. Getting tracked and listed on AgoraIQ is completely free. We make money from subscribers, not providers.
What do I need to get "Verified" status?
30+ tracked trades within 30 days, 60%+ data completeness (signals include entry, SL, TP), and no "Low" sample confidence rating.
Can I get delisted?
You can request removal at any time. We may also delist channels that stop posting or are found to be fraudulent.
Do you modify or censor my signals?
No. We ingest your signals as-is, hash them at the moment of ingestion, and track outcomes. We never modify signal content.
What if my channel format is unusual?
Our parser handles most common formats. Unusual formats may start with lower completeness but improve as we tune our parsers. We'll work with you.
