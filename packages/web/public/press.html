About AgoraIQ

AgoraIQ is a signal intelligence platform that tracks, verifies, and scores cryptocurrency trading signals from Telegram and Discord channels. The platform uses SHA-256 immutable proof hashing to eliminate cherry-picking and provide transparent, auditable performance records.

Founded in 2024 and operated by ChatLogic Insights LTD, AgoraIQ tracks 100+ signal providers and has processed over 50,000 signals with outcome verification against real market data from major exchanges.
